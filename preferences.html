My Favourite Dishes

Chicken Makhani
चिकन मखनी

A sweet, mild dish with delicious sauce that makes one's mouth water. This dish is my first choice whenever I'm ordering from an Indian restaurant, along with pilau rice which completes the dish.

Paratha
पराठा

Made from a dough of wheat flour, water, and oil, which is rolled out into a thin, circular shape and then cooked on a griddle or in a pan.

Peshwari Naan
पेश्वरी नान

A sweet dough bread with almonds and sultanas. I usually treat this as dessert after my main course whenever I get it.
