Starters

Vegetable Samosa
सब्जी समोसा

A traditional Indian starter consisting of triangular shaped pastry filled with mildly spiced vegetables.

Onion Bhajee
प्याज भाजी

Sliced onion, mixed with herbs and gram flour, then deep-fried until crispy golden.

Chicken Pakora
चिकन पकोड़ा

Sliced breast of chicken dipped in a spicy batter and deep-fried.

Peshwari Kebab
पेश्वरी कबाब

Minced lamb, delicately spiced and pan-fried.

Tandoori Mixed Meat Platter
तंदूरी मांस थाली

A combination platter of tandoori chicken, chicken tikka and lamb seekh kebab.

Mains

Chicken Tikka Masala
चिकन टिक्का मसाला

One of the most popular dishes in Indian restaurants. Cooked in mild, creamy sauce with tomatoes and almond powder.

Tandoori King Prawns
तंदूरी राजा झींगे

Marinated and cooked in a clay oven, the Tandoori King Prawns is a sizzling dish that certainly excites the taste buds!

Lamb Handi
लैम्ब हांडी

Lamb cooked on the bone with onion, garlic, ginger, black pepper, tumeric, chilli, tomato, and coriander. This dish involves very old style Indian cooking.

Chicken Tikka Biryani
चिकन टिक्का बिरयानी

Cooked with basmati rice, mild spices, mixed peppers, sultanas and almond flakes, Chicken Tikka Biryani is a smoky dish which consists of basmati rice and pieces of chicken.

Chicken Vindaloo
चिकन विंदालू

One of the hottest dishes served in restaurants, the Chicken Vindaloo is made from red chilli paste and a special blend of spices. It is cooked with chillies, potatoes and a touch of lemon.

Sides

Poppadoms
पापड़

Originating from India, the poppadom is a circular crisp bread made from flour and water that is fried in oil.

Rice
चावल

Rice, a staple of Indian cuisine. No dish is truly complete without some rice as an accompaniment.
There are several types of rice offered in Indian eateries nowadays:

Naan Bread
नान

Cooked in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven, naan bread is soft and slightly chewy, with a slightly puffed, bubbly texture. There are various types of naan bread too: garlic, peshwari, cheese, onion, keema, and more.

Lobia Masala
लोबिया मसाला

Black eyed beans cooked with garlic, ginger, onion and tomatoes. This side is gently spiced with tumeric, chilli and coriander powder and cumin also.

Alu Saag
आलू साग

A healthy curry made from potato cooked in a spinach sauce.
